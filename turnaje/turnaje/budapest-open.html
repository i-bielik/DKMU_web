Budapest Open 2015

DKMU reprezentovali na turnaji: Filip Vytřísal, Jakub Kobela, Ondrej Schutz

Podívejte se na výsledky řečníků a týmů.

Turnajové tézy:

  • This House would decrease the power of an individual's vote as the voter ages.
  • This House would ban all advertising of consumer goods and services.
  • This House opposes political correctness.
  • This House would approve drilling of oil and gas only based on a referendum.
  • This House would place a disincentivizing tax on businesses that use freelance labor.
  • SEMI-FINAL MOTION: This House would ban religions and ideologies from promising any form of rewards or punishments in the afterlife for any behaviour in this life.
  • FINAL MOTION: This House believes that the theory of communism is immoral.

Budapest Open 2016

DKMU reprezentovali na turnaji: Filip Vytřísal, Ivana Ivanová, Petr Gongala, Stanislav Krištofík, Tereza Gajdová, Tomáš Šakal

Podívejte se na výsledky řečníků a týmů.

Turnajové tézy:

  • This House would ban private political donations, and fund all political parties and campaigns from general taxation.
  • This House would not allow workers to renounce their employment rights (e.g. holiday pay, defined working hours, sick pay, minimum wage) in return for work experience or financial benefit.
  • This House believes that Oskar Gröning should not have been prosecuted.
  • This House believes that European Leaders should not have agreed to renegotiate the terms of Britain's EU membership.
  • This House believes that it is legitimate to release the unpublished personal memoirs of deceased public figures, even when this is against the express wishes of the individual or their family.
  • QUARTERFINAL MOTION: This House supports the French government's decision to dismantle the "Calais jungle."
  • SEMI-FINAL MOTION: This House believes that Barack Obama should nominate an individual considered to be a moderate conservative by US citizens to the Supreme Court.
  • FINAL MOTION: This House would ban all further research into artificial intelligence that can independently learn and develop.

Budapest Open 2017
