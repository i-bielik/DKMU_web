Na tyhle ročnících jsme byli

2016

European University Debating Competition 2016

DKMU reprezentovali na turnaji: Tereza Gajdová, Filip Vytřísal

Podívejte se na výsledky.

Turnajové tézy:

  • This House regrets the rise of social media as a primary source of news distribution.
  • This House supports Barack Obama's statements that emphasise African- Americans taking individual responsibility for improving their own position in life.
  • This House would create state-run centres where individuals can purchase and consume psychedelic drugs (drugs whose primary effect is to alter cognition and perception, e.g. LSD and Magic Mushrooms).
  • This House supports strong government intervention to prevent gentrification process when significant numbers of wealthier people move to a poor area.
  • This House believes that the Israeli Philharmonic Orchestra should regularly play the works of Richard Wagner.
  • This House regrets the mass commercialisation of football.
  • This House supports Sanctuary Cities in the United States.
  • This House believes that Latin American states should provide extensive material support for a military coup against the Venezuelan government.
  • This House believes that the European Union should fund satirical publications in its members states that target far-right political parties.
  • QUARTERFINAL MOTION: This house believes that Western States should fund the spread of atheism in Iraq through provision of education, health care and welfare.
  • SEMI-FINAL MOTION: This house regrets the safe space movements attempts to significantly limit speech on university campuses.
  • FINAL MOTION: This house believes that western states should support breakaway regions in former Soviet states (e.g. East Ukraine, South Ossetia) joining the Russian federation conditional on holding free and fair referenda.
